Frequently Asked Questions

Car mechanics diagnose, repair, and maintain vehicles, ensuring optimal performance and safety on the road. When facing car issues, consult certified mechanics for expert solutions and peace of mind.

  • What is MDmechanic?

    MDmechanic is your best friend when it comes to any car services! With efficient pricing, highly skilled experts, the latest equipment and technology and genuine parts, we bring you a range of services designed to keep your car in the best shape.

  • How can you offer upto 40% savings on services?

    Our uniquely efficient business model ensures that we offer affordable car services. Through it, we save on labor costs, and with centralized bulk procurement of spare parts, no real-estate overheads, and adept operational excellence, we’re able to give you the best for the least!

  • How is MDmechanic different from the other platforms out there?

    At MDmechanic, we’re not a lead generation focused business – you and your car are our first priorities. Satisfied, happy customers with smoothly functioning cars are our goals, and so we believe in being hands-on for the complete experience right from procurement of spare parts to the actual servicing, ensuring quality control. We send you prompt personalized updates about your car and are completely transparent about the pricing and quality of our services.

  • How do I book service on MDmechanic website ?
